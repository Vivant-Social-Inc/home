How it Works

Our network of hosts provide safe, compliant, and fun spaces where you can publically consume cannabis. Our network of performers provide the experience. All you have to do is pick the events you want.

Locations work with Vivant to obtain the proper licensure to host events. Once they are good to go, they will post their events here.
